Real-World Problems

Checksum protocols - Existed a long time to verify files and error-recovery to a minimal degree

Man-in-the-Middle Attack - Checksums are often store as plain text in htmls. Easy for malicious actors to manipulate and spoof bad files with “good” checksum

Wannacry Ransomware Attack -Affected more than 200,000 computers across 150 countries, with total damages ranging from hundreds of millions to billions of dollars

Solution

Storing the cryptographic signature of documents on decentralized database Ensure the system works with any file transfer

Tracking the change of hash on open blockchains
